C4 — Factory Vaastu Report

Industrial zones are genuinely different

The zone analysis for a factory is completely different from a home report: Production zone (SE — fire element for machinery), Raw Material storage (SW — earth element stability), Finished Goods dispatch (NW — Vayu zone for movement). These are real, distinct Vaastu principles for industrial layouts. The report must reflect this specificity — not just relabel "bedroom" as "production area".

Shruti's verdict for a business owner

Her quote is business-outcome language: "improve dispatch cycle times." Not "positive energy" or "prosperity" — specific, measurable, operational. Business owners respond to operational outcomes, not spiritual language. Shruti's note for a factory owner should sound like a management consultant, not a pandit.

Upsell to consultation — different price point

Factory consultation is ₹1,999 (vs ₹699 for home quick call). Business owners accept higher prices for their business than their home. The upsell CTA for industrial reports should always be for a full consultation — factories are too complex for a 20-min quick call.
