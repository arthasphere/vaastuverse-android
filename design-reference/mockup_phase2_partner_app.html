G1 — Guruji Home Dashboard

Dark theme for Gurujis

No credits section

G4 — Guruji Earnings Screen

Three income streams

  • Revenue share (₹4–8/report) — passive income from every report the Guruji's knowledge powers. Rate scales with tier: T2 earns ₹4/report, T1 earns ₹6–8/report based on contribution density
  • Consultation sessions — direct ₹2,499 (full flat) or ₹799 (quick) bookings routed to this Guruji
  • Quality review bonus — percentage uplift on revenue share based on customer ratings. Incentivises quality, not just volume

Customer rating drives bonus tier

Ratings from verified customer feedback (post-report survey) determine the bonus tier: 3.5–4.0★ = no bonus · 4.0–4.5★ = +5% · 4.5+★ = +15%. This directly links Guruji earnings to knowledge quality — not just quantity of entries submitted.

Revenue share calculation

₹4–8 per report sounds small, but at 2,341 reports influenced = ₹11,200/month passive. This grows exponentially as the Guruji adds more knowledge entries and the platform scales. Framing: "your knowledge earns while you sleep."

D2 — Designer Credit Management

Two clearly separated credit sections

Render credits (purple, top) and Vaastu credits (teal, below divider) are visually distinct. Different headings, different colours, different UX flows — never conflate them in the same card.

Vaastu credits are a one-time pack purchase (5/10/20 packs, discount at volume). "Optional" badge makes it clear this is an add-on. ₹249–299/credit vs ₹499 retail = strong designer incentive.

D4 — Designer Earnings Screen

Three income streams

  • Render margin — designer charges client ₹5,000–8,000 per render project, pays VaastuVerse ₹1,499/mo subscription. Gross margin per project typically ₹3,500–6,500.
  • Referral commissions — ₹1,600 per consultation booking the designer refers via their unique link
  • Quality bonus — % uplift on referral commissions based on verified client render ratings

Rating drives bonus tier (different from Guruji)

Designer bonus applies to referral commissions (not render margin): 4.0–4.5★ = +10% · 4.5+★ = +20%. Rating is based on client reviews of the renders delivered. This incentivises designers to deliver quality renders, not just volume — keeping the VaastuVerse brand standard high.

Referral link = passive income

Teal info card explains the referral mechanism simply. Designers naturally mention VaastuVerse to clients who ask about Vaastu — they now have financial incentive to formalise this with their link.
